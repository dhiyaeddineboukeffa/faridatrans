Project Farida: Transit Navigator

Project Farida is a smart transit navigation application designed to help people get around Constantine, Algeria, easily and efficiently.

Key Features:

1.  Interactive Map
    - View the entire transit network on a clear, easy-to-read map.
    - See the real-time locations of trams, buses, and taxis moving across the city.
    - Locate all transit stops and stations at a glance.

2.  Smart Routing
    - Simply enter your starting point and destination to get the best route.
    - The app calculates the quickest path, combining walking, trams, buses, and taxis.
    - It provides a step-by-step itinerary, telling you exactly where to walk, which line to take, and where to get off.

3.  Trip Details
    - Get a clear summary of your trip, including the total estimated time and the number of transfers.
    - See a detailed breakdown of each leg of your journey.

4.  Live Updates (Simulation)
    - The system simulates a live transit network, showing vehicles moving in real-time, so you can see exactly where your ride is.

Goal:
To make public transportation in Constantine accessible, predictable, and stress-free for everyone.
